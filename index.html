AutoList for Etsy

Automatically upload your ready-to-sell materials as Etsy listings — fast and easy.

What is AutoList?

AutoList is a productivity tool that automates the process of creating Etsy listings from your ready-made digital or physical products. Whether you're a digital creator, print-on-demand seller, or batch producer, AutoList saves you time by handling repetitive listing tasks.

Key Features

  • Automatically upload product data and images to Etsy
  • Map templates for pricing, categories, tags, and shipping
  • Supports batch uploads of digital and physical items
  • Integrates with your local storage, cloud, or CMS

How It Works

  1. Connect your Etsy shop via the Etsy API
  2. Set up your listing templates and product folders
  3. Click "Upload" — AutoList generates and posts listings

Who It's For

  • Etsy sellers with large digital libraries (PDFs, templates, art)
  • Print-on-demand creators managing hundreds of SKUs
  • Teams managing Etsy as a sales channel
